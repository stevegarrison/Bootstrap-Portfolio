Steve Garrison

Financial Analyst | Developer

Steve Garrison is a Colorado native, where he recently graduated from the University of Denver's full-stack web-development coding bootcamp in May 2019, and holds a degree in Finance from Colorado State University where he graduated in December 2018. Steve held a number of leadership roles during his tenure at CSU, and was a Dean's List student. In June 2019 Steve moved to Salt Lake City to begin his career as a Financial Analyst with Goldman Sachs on the Finance Divison as a Product Controller on the Investment Management Division.

Steve is passionate about the financial markets, developement and the integration between the two, and hopes to use his knowledge of the both to increase the accuracy, depth and efficiency of financial analysis.

In his free time, Steve loves learning new things, skiing, golfing, cooking and eating, and spending time with friends and family!
